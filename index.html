  • Lemes ka Sorangan: Soft word refers to self. e.g. I eat = Abdi neda
  • >Lemes ka Batur: Soft word refers to show respect to someone. e.g. Father eat = Bapa tuang
  • Basa Loma: Generic words used to chat with friends. e.g. David eats = David dahar
  • Basa Indonesia: Bahasa Indonesia translation
  • Basa Inggris: English translation
